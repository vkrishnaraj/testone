Welcome to Net-Tracer™

Net-Tracer is the 21st century solution to Baggage Tracing, Claims and Management.  The Owens Group has developed a web-based application for the purpose of assisting commercial airlines with the management of mishandled passenger baggage.  The solution, Net-Tracer, is a subscription-based Internet service for baggage tracing and claims resolution.  By using Net-Tracer, baggage service personnel are given a user-friendly web-based interface that minimizes training and permits cross-utilization of station personnel.
