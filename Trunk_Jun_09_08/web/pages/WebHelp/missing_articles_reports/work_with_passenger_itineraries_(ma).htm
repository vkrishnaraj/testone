Work with Passenger Itineraries

 

 

The Passenger Itinerary section allows you to enter the flight information for each segment of the passenger’s journey.

 

The origination (From) and destination (To) city, airline code, flight number and departure date are required for each segment. Optional fields include the arrival date of the flight, Scheduled Departure Time, Scheduled Arrival Time, Actual Departure Time and Actual Arrival Time.

 

To add an additional Passenger Itinerary to a Missing Articles report:

  1. Create a new Missing Articles report or locate and view an existing one.
     

  2. Click the button to jump to the Passenger and Baggage Itinerary sections of the report.
     

  3. Click to add another Passenger Itinerary to the report.
     

  4. Enter the routing and flight information for each segment of the passenger itinerary.
     

  5. Move on to the next section or scroll to the end of the report and click to accept the changes.
     

To edit an existing Passenger Itinerary in a Missing Articles report:

  1. Locate and view an existing Missing Articles report.
     

  2. Click the button to jump to the Passenger and Baggage Itinerary sections of the report.
     

  3. Make the desired changes to the Passenger Itinerary.
     

  4. Scroll to the end of the report and click to accept the changes.
     

To delete a Passenger Itinerary from a Missing Articles report:

  1. Locate and view an existing Missing Articles report.
     

  2. Click the button to jump to the Passenger and Baggage Itinerary sections of the report.
     

  3. Click to remove the desired passenger itinerary.
     

  4. Scroll to the end of the report and click to accept the changes.
