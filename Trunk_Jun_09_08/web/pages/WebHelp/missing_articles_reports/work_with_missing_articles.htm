Work with Missing Articles

 

 

Detailed information must be added for each article that the passenger is reporting as missing. The Missing Articles section contains descriptive details about all missing articles including each item's description and cost.

 

To add an additional missing article to a Missing Articles report:

  1. Create a new Damaged Bag report or locate and view an existing one.
     

  2. Click the button to jump to the Missing Articles section of the report.
     

  3. Click to add an another article to the report.
     

  4. Enter the optional and required information in the appropriate fields.
     

  5. Move on to the next section or scroll to the end of the report and click to accept the changes.

Note

 

There is no limit to the number of damaged bags that can be added to a Damaged Bag report.

 

 

To edit an existing article in a Missing Articles report:

  1. Locate and view an existing Missing Articles report.
     

  2. Click the button to jump to the Missing Articles section of the report.
     

  3. Make the desired changes to the missing article's information fields.
     

  4. Scroll to the end of the report and click to accept the changes.
     

To delete an article from a Missing Articles report:

  1. Locate and view an existing Missing Articles report.
     

  2. Click the button to jump to the Missing Articles section of the report.
     

  3. Click the button that corresponds with the article that is to be deleted.
     

  4. Scroll to the end of the report and click to accept the changes.

Important

 

A minimum of one Missing Article is required to complete a report.

 

 

Missing Articles Fields

Note

 

An asterisk (*) indicates the field is a required field.

 

Tip

 

Text box fields can be edited by typing over the existing data with new data. Dropdown list fields can be changed by moving your cursor over the new option to highlight it and clicking to enter the new data in the field.

 

Field Name

Field Description

Article

Place your cursor in the Article field and enter the type of the item that the passenger is claiming to be missing from their baggage.

Description

Enter a brief description of the missing article and any other information that might be relevant to process the report.

Purchase Date

Enter the original purchase date for the missing article that the passenger is claiming.

Cost

Enter the total dollar amount that the passenger is claiming for the original cost of the missing article.

Currency

Select the currency type for the cost value the passenger is claiming by pointing to the currency code in the drop-down list and clicking on the selected currency.
