Work with Claim Prorate Notices

 

 

Two options are available from this screen, Claim Resolution and Claim Prorate.

 

The Claim Prorate function only applies to an airline’s central claims office and is normally administered by a Prorate Officer. The user must be assigned to an Admin, LZ or Claim group in order to access this section.
