How Do I Print a Prorate Notice

 

 

Click on Print Prorate Notice in the upper right hand corner of the screen to print a copy of the prorate notice.

 

To print a passenger receipt:

  1. Create and save/submit a new report.

    - or -

    Locate and view and existing report that has been saved or submitted.
     

  2. Click the link from the toolbar that appears in the upper right corner of the report.
     

  3. When the receipt appears as a PDF file, choose Print from the File menu.
