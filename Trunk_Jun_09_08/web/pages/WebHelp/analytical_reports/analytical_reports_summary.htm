Analytical Reports

 

 

Analytical Reports are designed to provide management with detailed information which may be used to assist in managing baggage mishandlings.  An asterisk (*) indicates the field is a required field.

 

The following options are available by selecting the Analytical Reports option from the main menu bar:

  • Occurrences

  • By Passenger Boarding

  • Occurrences Per Flight

  • Disbursements

  • Station

  • Recovery
