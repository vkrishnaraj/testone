Working with Claims

 

 

Two options are available from this screen, Claim Resolution and Claim Prorate.

 

Claim Resolution

Payment or settlement information may not be entered until the record is saved and a NetTracer Record Number established. Display the record by going to the Display header on the navigation bar. Moving the cursor over Display will display a drop down menu. Select Display Report and enter the file reference number you want to display. Move the cursor to Display and click. After the record is displayed select Claims from the navigation bar or scroll to the bottom of the record.

Note

Claim Form Fields

Field Name

Field Description

Claim Amount

In this area the user is given the option of recording the Amount Claimed by the customer.

Tip

 

A user may also select a Claim Status from the drop down box.

 

Payment Summary

This area gives the user a quick review of previous payments.  NetTracer provides the user with a summary of previous payments starting with the date the payment was made.  This is followed by the Payment Location, Payment Type, payment Code, Draft Number, the date the Draft was requested, the date the Draft was issued and the Draft amount.  Additional information includes the amount of any Vouchers or Mileage issued and Comments explaining the payment.

Add Payment

The user is given several options in the Add Payment section.

Date

The Date the payment section is completed is automatically entered by NetTracer.

Payment Location

Location is selected from the drop down box.  The options are Station, CBS (LZ) or Claim Office.

Payment Type

Payment Type is also selected from a drop down box.  Here the options are Damage, Delivery, Interim, Goodwill, Loss, Missing Articles, Unchecked Lost and Found or Other.

Draft Number

Draft Number is the area where the user will record the Draft or Check number.

Draft Request Date

Draft Request Date is provided for record keeping.  This is important when one office requests the payment and the draft/check is issued from another location.

Draft Issued Date

Draft Issued Date provides the user with an audit trail of when the draft or check was actually issued.

Draft Amount

Draft Amount will be the amount of payment being sent to the customer.

Currency

Currency gives the user the ability to record payment in various world currencies.

Voucher Amount

Voucher Amount and Mileage Amount entries are provided to record payments other than check or cash.

Code

The user may Code the payment from the drop down box using options of Advanced (Interim Charges), Delivery (Delivery Costs), Final (Final Settlement), Insurance (To record payments made by your insurance carrier or Risk Management office) and Other.

Comments

A Comments section is provided to record additional information regarding the claim settlement.

Save *

The user must click Save to enter Payment information into the record.

Claim Prorate *

This function only applies to an airline’s central claims office and is normally administered by a Prorate Officer.  The user must be assigned to an Admin, LZ or Claim group in order to access this section.
