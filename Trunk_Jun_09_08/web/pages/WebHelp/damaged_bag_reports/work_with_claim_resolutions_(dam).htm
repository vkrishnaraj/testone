Work with Claim Resolutions

 

 

Two options are available from this screen, Claim Resolution and Claim Prorate.

 

The top portion of the screen displays the Claim Amount. This section is used when the passenger made a claim for reimbursement of losses due to mishandling of their checked baggage. Generally Claim Amount will require further research or processing before payment is issued. Payment or settlement information may not be entered until the record is saved and a NetTracer Record Number is established.

 

Examples of Claim Amount may include:

  • A claim for permanent loss of checked baggage
     

  • A claim for damage baggage that can not be repaired to the customers satisfaction
     

  • A claim for items that a passenger alleged are missing due damage to their baggage
     

  • A claim for items that a passenger alleges are missing from their checked baggage
     

To add a Claim Resolution to a Damaged Bag report:

  1. Create and save a new Damaged Bag report.
     

  2. Select the Report Number link that is shown on the save confirmation screen.
     

  3. From the section toolbar, click to display the Claim Resolution screen.
     

  4. Enter the Claim Amount information and add any payments made on the claim.
     

  5. Click to add the Claim Resolution to the report.

Note

 

It does not matter whether or not Saved As Temporary or Submit for Tracing is chosen as the method to save the report.

 

 

Claim Amount Fields

Note

 

An asterisk (*) indicates the field is a required field.

 

Tip

 

Text box fields can be edited by typing over the existing data with new data. Dropdown list fields can be changed by moving your cursor over the new option to highlight it and clicking to enter the new data in the field.

 

Field Name

Field Description

Claim Amount

In this area the user is given the option of recording the Amount Claimed by the customer.

Claim Status

Optionally, a status for the claim can be chosen from the drop-down menu.

Currency

Currency gives the user the ability to record payment in various world currencies.

Passenger Name

Enter the passenger’s last name, first name and middle initial. Partial information may be entered if the full name is not available. Middle initial is not required.

Social Security Number

Enter the passenger's Social Security number. This field is used primarily by an airline's baggage claims officer.

Reason for Change (For Audit Purposes)

Enter comments or additional details up to 255 characters. The text box will count down from 255 to 0 as you type information into the field.
