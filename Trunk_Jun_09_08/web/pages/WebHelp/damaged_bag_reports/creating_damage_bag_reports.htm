Create & Work with Damaged Bag Reports

There are seven sections and/or types of information that can be collected and entered when creating a new Damaged Bag report. The Report Information section is at the top of the report and houses general information and tracking information about the report itself including the Agent submitting the report and its current status.

 

The Passenger Information and Passenger Itinerary sections include contact and travel information about the person(s) filing the claim. The Baggage Itinerary, Damaged Bag Information and Baggage Check Information sections list the details about the individual bags that are part of the claim, the specific damage and possibly digital photos of the bags themselves. Finally, the Remarks section allows space for any additional comments that do not fit into any of the other supplied fields or sections.
