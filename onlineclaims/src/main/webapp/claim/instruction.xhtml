CUSTOMER INSTRUCTIONS

Please accept our sincere apology for the mishandling of your property. We understand your concern and realize the inconvenience this causes. US Airways will make every effort to merit your future business by handling your report in an efficient and equitable manner.


Tracing of your property began as soon as you reported the mishandling to our agent. While our efforts to locate your property continue, it is imperative that you complete this claim form and submit it to us within 45 days from the date you initially reported the mishandling. Failure to complete and submit your claim form may result in denial of this claim. The most intensive phase of tracing is based on the accuracy of the information provided and completed in its entirety with a detailed description of each individual item contained within the bag(s) or the items that are missing.


If your claim involves more than one piece of baggage, please itemize each bag and its contents separately. Failure to include the requested information may affect the processing of your claim.

  • Customer copy of the flight ticket or E-Ticket receipt, along with complete flight itinerary
  • Claim form must be signed by all affected customers
  • Original proof of purchase for items valued at $150 USD or higher
  • Baggage claim checks
  • Excess value receipt, if excess value was declared
  • Original receipts are required for all reimbursement for delay expenses
  • Legible copy of identification (i.e. driver's license, passport)
  • Damage claims: Retain all damaged bags and/or contents until your claim has been resolved
  • Please retain a copy of all documents sent to US Airways for your personal records
